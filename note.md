# Auth & Onboarding

1. /login – Cashier / Manager login (demo accounts)
2. /register – Store owner signup (demo only)

# Cashier Flow (most used)

3. /dashboard – Today’s sales, top drinks, quick stats + quick new order button
4. /pos – Main POS terminal (category tabs: Signature, Milk Tea, Fruit Tea, Toppings, Snacks)
5. /pos/customize/:drinkId – Deep drink customizer (size, sweetness, ice, toppings, base swap, notes) with live price calculator + bubble animation
6. /pos/cart – Order summary with quantity edit, discounts, notes
7. /pos/payment – Payment methods (Cash, Card, QR Pay, Apple Pay, Store Credit) + tip screen
8. /pos/receipt – Digital receipt preview + print simulation + SMS/Email option

# Order Management

9. /orders – Order history list (filter by date, status, cashier)
10. /orders/:orderId – Detailed order view + refund button

# Manager / Back Office

11. /inventory – Stock overview (ingredients + cups + toppings) with low-stock alerts
12. /inventory/adjust – Quick stock adjustment form
13. /menu – Full product catalog (view, edit, hide items)
14. /menu/new – Add new drink / snack form (with image upload simulation)
15. /staff – Employee list + clock-in/out simulation
16. /staff/new – Add/edit staff (role: cashier/manager/owner)

# Analytics & Settings

17. /reports – Sales reports (daily/weekly/monthly) + charts (Recharts)
18. /customers – Loyalty program members + points history
19. /settings – Store info, tax rates, printer settings, theme switcher
20. /settings/promotions – Create flash sales / happy hour discounts (bonus page)
